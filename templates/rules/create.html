Field Condition Tips
  • Use pcre2 for most conditions — supports full Perl regex syntax
  • Wrap values in (?i)(value1|value2) for case-insensitive OR matching
  • Check negate to exclude events where the field matches
  • Multiple field conditions are evaluated with AND logic
  • Common fields: win.eventdata.commandLine, win.eventdata.parentImage, win.system.eventID
Frequency / Timeframe Notes
  • frequency — number of matching events required to trigger (2 to 9999)
  • timeframe — time window in seconds (1 to 99999)
  • ignore — seconds to ignore rule after it fires (1 to 999999)
  • Requires if_matched_sid or if_matched_group when used
